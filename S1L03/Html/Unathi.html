Biography and Background

Unathi was born in Mpumalanga, and went to school in St Marys. She studies BCom Informatics with Business as an elective and would want to be a business analyst in the future.

Hobbies

Unathi likes to paint and build sculptures. She likes painting because it allows for more freedom in manipulating color, light, and perspective on a flat surface.She also likes sculpting because sculptures often provide a three-dimensional experience that engages viewers from multiple angles, allowing for a unique interaction with the artwork. The physicality of sculptures can evoke a sense of presence and can be more impactful in spatial environments.
